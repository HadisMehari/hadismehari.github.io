Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie.

One 9-inch pie
30 Min - Prep time
10 Min - Cook time
40 Min - Total
8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 Cup white Suger
  • 2 tbsp all purpose floour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg yoks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie curst, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp white suger

DIRECTIONS

  1. preheared Oven: preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C)
  2. Make lemon Filling: in a medium saucepan ...
    • Whisk togother 1 cup suger, flour, cornstarch, and salt
    • Stir in water, lemon juice and lemon zest
    • Cook over medium-high heat, strring frequently, until mixture comes to a boil
    • Stir butter
    • Place egg yolks in small bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot suger mixture
    • Whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining suger mixture
    • Bring to a boil and continue to cook while stirring constantly until thick
    • Remove from heat
    • Pour filling into baked pastery shell
  3. Make Meringgue: In Large glass or metal bowl ...
    • Whip egg whites until foamy
    • Add sugar gradually, and continue to whipuntill stiff peaks form
    • Spread Meringue over pie, sealing the edges at the curst..
  4. Bake: Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until meringue is golden brown
